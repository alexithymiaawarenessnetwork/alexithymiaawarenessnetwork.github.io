# Crisis & Urgent Support

This page has been condensed into [Support](support.md#urgent-support).

If you are in immediate danger, call 911 (US) or your local emergency number now.

For suicide, self-harm, or acute distress resources, go to:

- [Support → Urgent Support](support.md#urgent-support)

Quick US contacts:
- **988** — call or text
- **Crisis Text Line** — text **HOME** to **741741**
- **911** — emergency services

For international crisis lines, LGBTQ+ specific resources, veterans support, and the contextual notes previously on this page, use the integrated section on [Support](support.md#urgent-support).
