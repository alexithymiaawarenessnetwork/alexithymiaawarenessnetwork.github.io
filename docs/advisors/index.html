Advisors

How to contact AAN about advisory, review, or collaboration support.

AAN is not publishing a named advisor roster on this page.

If you are interested in advising

If you are a researcher, clinician, educator, community organizer, or person with relevant lived experience who would like to support AAN’s educational work, please email team@alexithymiaawarenessnetwork.org.

Helpful context to include:

  • your area of experience or expertise;
  • what kind of review or support you are offering;
  • any public profile or affiliation you want AAN to use when checking details;
  • whether you would want your name listed publicly if a public advisor page is created later.

Public listing boundaries

AAN will not publish advisor names, roles, affiliations, or endorsement-style language without consent and current public details.
