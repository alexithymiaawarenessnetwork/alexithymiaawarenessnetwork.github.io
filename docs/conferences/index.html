Conferences & Events

How AAN handles event listings relevant to alexithymia, emotion research, education, and support.

AAN does not publish a general conference calendar or endorsement list.

What belongs here

A conference or event can be listed when AAN has enough current information to verify the entry. Each listing should include:

  • the official event name and link;
  • dates, location, and whether online attendance is available;
  • the organizing society or host organization;
  • submission, registration, or accessibility notes when relevant and verified;
  • a short, plain-language note about why the event may matter for alexithymia, emotion research, support, education, or related clinical/research communities.

What AAN does not list

This page should not publish:

  • standing conference names without a current official event page;
  • stale calendars from earlier years;
  • claims that an event is “best,” “recommended,” or clinically useful without evidence;
  • sponsor-style language or copied promotional descriptions;
  • broad category lists that imply AAN has reviewed the event.

Suggest an event

If you know of a current conference, symposium, webinar series, or community event that may be relevant to alexithymia or emotional awareness, send the official link and a brief note about the connection.

AAN will review suggestions for relevance, source quality, date currency, accessibility, and fit with AAN’s informational, non-clinical role before listing them publicly.
