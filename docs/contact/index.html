Contact

How to reach the Alexithymia Awareness Network for non-urgent questions, corrections, and collaboration.

Use this page for non-urgent contact with the Alexithymia Awareness Network.

Email: team@alexithymiaawarenessnetwork.org

What to email about

You can use this address for:

  • questions or feedback about AAN pages;
  • source suggestions, citation corrections, or broken-link reports;
  • research, clinician, media, or collaboration inquiries;
  • brief personal-experience notes that may help AAN understand community needs.

If you are sending a correction or source suggestion, please include the page URL, the claim or link involved, and any source details that would help us verify it.

What email cannot do

AAN is informational and community-oriented. We cannot provide diagnosis, treatment, therapy, immediate-safety support, or personal medical advice by email.

Please avoid sending urgent safety concerns or highly sensitive medical details through this inbox. If your question is about your own mental health, assessment, or care options, a qualified clinician is the right person to consult.

Safety and clinical limits

Please do not use the AAN inbox for immediate safety, medical, or emergency needs. AAN is not equipped for that role.

For non-urgent support orientation, see Support.
