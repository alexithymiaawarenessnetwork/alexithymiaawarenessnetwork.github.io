Research Participation Studies

How AAN handles research participation listings related to alexithymia, emotional awareness, and adjacent topics.

This page is for research participation opportunities related to alexithymia, emotional awareness, interoception, and adjacent topics.

It is different from the Research Papers library. Papers are published research records; a paper citation does not mean a study is recruiting participants.

Listings

AAN does not list active research participation opportunities unless the listing can be verified from an official source.

A study listing should identify:

  • whether the study is active or recruiting;
  • the official link;
  • the institution, research team, or study sponsor;
  • eligibility criteria, location or remote-participation status, compensation if any, and contact details;
  • ethics, consent, or institutional-review information when applicable;
  • language that does not imply AAN endorsement or promise that participation will diagnose, treat, or explain a person’s alexithymia.

Why caution matters

Research opportunities can close, change eligibility rules, or move to a new contact link. A stale listing may waste a reader’s time or create false confidence about a study’s purpose.

Suggest a study

If you know of a current study that may be relevant to alexithymia or emotional awareness, send the official study link, institution or lab name, contact page, and any recruitment deadline to team@alexithymiaawarenessnetwork.org.

AAN will review suggestions for relevance, official-source verification, safety, accessibility, and fit with AAN’s informational role before listing them.

Important note

AAN does not run these studies unless a listing explicitly says so. Participation decisions are personal and voluntary. If you have questions about risks, privacy, compensation, eligibility, or whether participation is appropriate for you, contact the study team or a qualified professional.
