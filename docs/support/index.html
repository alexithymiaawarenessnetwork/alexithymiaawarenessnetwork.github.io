Support

Find directories, peer spaces, and practical supports.

Professional directories

Check fit directly with any provider: credentials, scope, availability, cost, privacy, telehealth options, and emergency-coverage policies.

  • Psychology Today therapist directory — psychologytoday.com/us/therapists
  • TherapyDen therapist directory — therapyden.com
  • Inclusive Therapists — inclusivetherapists.com
  • Open Path Collective — openpathcollective.org

Bring treatment questions to a qualified clinician. For background, read Treatment and Therapy Approaches.

Community and peer spaces

Before posting, check privacy, moderation, rules, and current activity.

  • r/Alexithymia — reddit.com/r/Alexithymia
  • AAN-hosted forum — alexithymia.us/forum

Practical supports

For workplace, school, family, relationship, disability, legal, or accommodation questions: gather concrete examples, then use the qualified support for that setting.

  • Job Accommodation Network — askjan.org
  • NAMI Support & Education — nami.org
  • Mental Health America: Finding Help — mhanational.org/finding-help

If distress is hard to name

Difficulty naming feelings is not proof that distress is absent (Luminet, Nielson, and Ridout, 2021). Body signals, unsafe urges, shutdown, panic, inability to sleep, or a sense of danger matter even when no exact emotion word fits.
