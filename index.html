Alexithymia? Exactly.

If you've never heard of Alexithymia, you're in good company. The condition, originally described by Peter Sifneos in 1973, is characterized by a difficulty in identifying and describing emotions. AAN exists to educate the public about Alexithymia and to provide resources for those who may be affected by it.
